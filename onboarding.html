LaborCoin Onboarding

This guide walks new users through the complete LaborCoin onboarding process. Follow these steps to access the exchange, register with the DAO, and participate in governance.

Before You Begin

LaborCoin operates on Polygon and uses Gitcoin Passport verification to help prevent duplicate accounts, governance abuse, and automated participation.

  • MetaMask wallet
  • Polygon Mainnet
  • Gitcoin Passport
  • A qualifying Passport score

Step 1: Install MetaMask

Install MetaMask and create a wallet.

Your wallet allows you to interact with the LaborCoin exchange, DAO registration system, and governance platform.

Store your recovery phrase securely. Never share your recovery phrase with anyone.

Download MetaMask

New to Crypto Wallets?

MetaMask is a self-custody wallet. You control your own funds and recovery phrase.

LaborCoin cannot recover lost recovery phrases or wallet access.

Step 2: Switch to Polygon

LaborCoin operates entirely on the Polygon network.

Ensure MetaMask is connected to Polygon Mainnet before attempting to use any LaborCoin feature.

Learn About Polygon

Step 3: Create a Gitcoin Passport

Visit Gitcoin Passport and connect your wallet.

Gitcoin Passport allows users to build a reputation score using verification methods known as "stamps."

LaborCoin uses Passport verification to support one verified identity per participant.

Open Gitcoin Passport

Step 4: Increase Your Passport Score

If your Passport score is too low, you may need additional verification.

Common ways to increase your score include:

  • Government ID verification
  • Phone verification
  • Social account verification
  • Additional Passport stamps

Passport requirements may evolve over time as anti-abuse protections improve.

Accessing the Exchange

Step 5: Buy LABR

Visit the LaborCoin Exchange.

Connect your wallet and complete Passport verification.

Once verified, you may purchase LABR through the on-chain bonding curve exchange.

Important

DAO registration is NOT required to purchase, hold, or sell LABR.

Exchange access and DAO membership are separate systems.

Joining the DAO

Step 6: Register with the DAO

DAO registration is optional.

Registration is only required if you want governance rights.

To register:

  • Hold at least 1 LABR
  • Complete Passport verification
  • Sign the LaborCoin Attestation
  • Complete DAO registration

Participating in Governance

Step 7: Use Your Voting Rights

Once registered, you receive LABRV governance voting rights.

LABRV holders may:

  • Create treasury proposals
  • Vote on proposals
  • Execute approved proposals during execution windows

Understanding LABR and LABRV

LABR

  • Transferable token
  • Purchased through the exchange
  • Subject to exchange limits and cooldowns
  • Used to build the shared treasury

LABRV

  • Non-transferable governance token
  • Granted through DAO registration
  • Represents voting rights
  • Used exclusively for governance participation

Key Distinctions

  • Gitcoin Passport verification and DAO registration are not the same thing.
  • Passport verification is used to access protected LaborCoin features.
  • DAO registration grants governance rights and LABRV voting power.
  • Buying LABR does not require DAO registration.
  • Governance participation requires DAO registration and LABRV.
