Governance Requirements

Governance participation requires:

  • DAO registration
  • LABRV voting rights
  • Passport verification

Anyone may view proposals. Any connected wallet may execute an approved proposal during its execution window. Creating and voting require LABRV and identity verification.

New users should review the onboarding guide before participating.
